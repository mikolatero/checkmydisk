CheckMyDisk can use smartctl from smartmontools.

smartmontools is free software distributed under the GNU General Public License.
Home page: https://www.smartmontools.org/

If a bundled smartctl binary is shipped with a release build, that release must
include the matching license notice and source-offer information required by GPL.

External USB/FireWire SMART access on macOS may require SATSMARTDriver or another
driver exposing SAT pass-through to smartctl. CheckMyDisk v1 detects support and
guides the user, but does not install kernel extensions.
